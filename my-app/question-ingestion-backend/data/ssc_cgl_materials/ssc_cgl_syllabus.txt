The SSC CGL exam syllabus covers multiple subjects across different tiers.

Tier I includes:
1. General Intelligence & Reasoning
2. General Awareness
3. Quantitative Aptitude
4. English Comprehension

Tier II includes:
1. Quantitative Abilities
2. English Language and Comprehension
3. Statistics
4. General Studies (Finance & Economics)

The SSC CGL exam is highly competitive and requires thorough preparation across all subjects.
